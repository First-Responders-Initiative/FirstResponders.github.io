The First Responders Initiative

What is FRI?

First Responders Initiative (FRI) is a unique approach to addressing the growing problem of dangerous forest fires. Our approach involves a 3-in-1 Hazard Detector sealed within discreet housing. Learn more about our design here.

Wildfire FAQs

1. How does the 3-in-1 Hazard Tool aid in the combatting of wildfires?

The 3-in-1 Hazard Tool has an Arduino Uno hooked up to 3 different sensors: an alcohol sensor, a flammable gas sensor, and a smoke sensor
